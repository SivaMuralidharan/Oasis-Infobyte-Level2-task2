BioGraphy

Allu Arjun is an actor, producer, director, and dancer who works in the South Indian cinema industry. When it comes to South Indian cinema, he is one of the first names that come to mind. His films are consistently superhits and are known among the greatest Telugu films. He is on the list of the highest-earning actors in the Tollywood industry.

With back-to-back box office successes, the actor has gotten attention, and as per Forbes India, Allu Arjun is one of the top 100 celebrities in the country. He has also been given a few nicknames based on the roles he played in his movies. Nicknames given to hin include "Bunny" and a "stylish star".

Arjun began his acting career in 2003 with the hit movie "Gangotri," although he had already worked as a child artist back in 1985. He acted in several films like 'Desamuduru', 'Happy', Rudhramadevi' 'Parugu', Lucky The Racer, and others. He was also awarded a Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut for his work in this film. Allu then featured in an immensely successful movie "Arya" in 2004. In addition, he was honored with the Nandi Award for Best Actor. Then another movie, "Bunny," featuring Allu, was released in 2005 and got the audience's intense love and gave him the nickname "Bunny". Recently a movie named "Pushpa" got released, and people loved it.
